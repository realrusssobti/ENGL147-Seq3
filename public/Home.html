Our Values

Automation

We strongly believe that automation with AI is a force for good. By increasing the safety of AI systems, humanity will be more productive as a whole and advance in a multitude of issues.

Equity

We believe that AI systems should be evaluated from the same criteria for diversity, equity, and inclusion as humans. When dealing with sensitive groups of people, care should be taken to make sure that AI is not unintentionally marginalizing people.

Objectivity

We believe that automated decision-making systems have the potential to improve the quality of decision-making across industries. Hooli believes that AI removes human biases when care is taken to avoid reproducing the same bias inside the dataset or AI model. 

Case Study: Tay.AI

In 2016, Microsoft unveiled a chatbot called Tay.ai, which was a trained Twitter chatbot that used cutting-edge deep learning technologies. While Tay was not directly used for business objectives, the users of Twitter were able to "troll" the AI. Due to a lack of data filtering, this led Tay to express racist, sexist, xenophobic, and highly controversial opinions. Tay was promptly shut down.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you unsure what AI is or what makes AI safe or unsafe? We've answered the basic questions you need to know to get the most out of our site.

What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is a type of application that tries to make decisions or control systems on its own. AI is a cutting-edge field of computer science, which has applications in any industry that decisions need to be made in. 

Believers in AI think that computers are more objective, and can make faster and more rational decisions than a human. Additionally, there is research being done where people use AI to find trends they otherwise would have missed. 

How do businesses and governments use AI?

Businesses use AI to reduce costs and increase efficiencies across their supply chain. Business use cases of AI are based on the following questions:

  • Yield Prediction : How can I know how much product will be made from some inputs?
  • Engineering Design Automation : How can I predict how this design performs?
  • Resource Allocation: What is the best use of limited resources?
  • Business Intelligence: What trends exist in the organization's data?

AI cannot solve all problems, but it can be an invaluable tool when used properly. 

How are AI and cybersecurity related?

AI systems are a type of application for a computer. As with any other type of app, this means that AI models must be designed and used with cybersecurity in mind. Cybersecurity for the AI model involves the same principles of confidentiality, integrity, and accessibility as other apps. In addition to securing the application itself, the data and models that AI systems use are also vulnerable. Hooli believes that this supply chain must also be protected to ensure safe, responsible, and reliable use of AI.
